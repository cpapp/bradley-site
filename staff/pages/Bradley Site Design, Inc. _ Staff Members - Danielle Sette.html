Danielle

Sette

Production Manager | APM

Bio

Originally from New Jersey, Danielle began her career with Bradley Site Design after graduating with honors from the nationally ranked Penn State program in 2014. During her academic career, Danielle won numerous design awards for stormwater management and design excellence. She also studied abroad in New Zealand at Lincoln University in Canterbury, where she gained knowledge in ecotourism and revitalizing urban cores post-natural disasters.

Since joining BSD, Danielle has assisted on multiple DC Public School projects and has taken the lead on county-wide stormwater management projects. Danielle specializes in horticulture and planting design, but also brings advanced skills in document production, grading, construction detailing, graphics and design theory.

Education

Minor of Environmental Inquiry Focused in Environmental Explorations
Minor of International Studies
Study Abroad, School of Landscape Architecture // Lincoln University, Canterbury, New Zealand (2013)
