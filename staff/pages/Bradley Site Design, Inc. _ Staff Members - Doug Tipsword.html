Doug

Tipsword

Technical Director | PM

Bio

Doug possesses nearly 10 years of experience in various disciplines of landscape design, graphic communication, and construction. The body of his work as a landscape architect includes urban plazas, streetscapes, roof top amenities, public parks, embassies, commercial centers and multi-family developments. Doug has worked as a project manager for a broad variety of projects with differing scales, scopes and complexities. His design approach as a landscape architect has always been one of balance between aesthetics and functionality. Furthermore, he strongly believes that an acute attention to detail while also keeping the overall design objectives closely in perspective often results in the most creative design solutions.

Education

Masters of Landscape Architecture Auburn University, 2006
Bachelor of Science in Environmental Design, Auburn University, 2004

REGISTRATIONS / ACCREDITATIONS 

Registered Landscape Architect – Virginia 

Interests

Doug loves dogs, camping, snowboarding, fishing, genealogy research, grilling, and torturing himself by supporting DC sports teams
