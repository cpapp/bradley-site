Gwen

Wolfgang

Landscape Architecture Designer

Bio

Gwen’s background in architecture (Clemson University 2007), landscape architecture (University of Georgia 2012), and sustainable design (LEED AP Legacy) has resulted in a strong design approach that considers needs of both stakeholders and the environment. She spent time studying in Italy and brings with her experience working with stakeholders and making public presentations, having worked with both in the public sector and with private residential developers.

Education

Bachelor of Arts in Architecture – Clemson University, 2007
Master of Landscape Architecture – University of Georgia, 2012
LEED AP Legacy
Professional Member – American Society of Landscape Architects

Interests

Outside of the office, Gwen enjoys eating (and occasionally attempting to make) good food, and she likes to spend time traveling and exploring new places with friends and family.

Fun Fact

I’ve been to Disney World/Disneyland over 35 times, and I already have my next trip planned!
