Hannah

Arensen

Landscape Architecture Designer

Bio

Hannah’s design interests revolve around how the construction of outdoor spaces impact human interaction, an interest that led her to Barcelona to study public space, as well as central New York where she took part in community design research. As a landscape designer, she likes to focus on function and dig into the details. Hannah’s inspiration comes primarily from her travels and volunteer work. She is particularly passionate about working with communities to create safe spaces that promote health, wellbeing and sustainability.

Education

Bachelor’s of Landscape Architecture - Magna Cum Laude - SUNY College of Environmental Science and Forestry

Interests

Music, travel, food, camping, fishing, anything outside.

Fun Fact

Despite being a landscape designer, Hannah is shockingly bad at keeping potted plants alive.
